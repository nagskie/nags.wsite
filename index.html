The University of Manila

About Us

Founded in 1913 by Apolinario G. de los Santos, Maria de los Santos, and Mariano V. de los Santos, The University of Manila (UM) began as the Instituto de Manila. Over more than a century, it has stood as a beacon of academic excellence in Sampaloc, surviving the challenges of history to produce thousands of leaders and professionals who serve the Filipino nation.
